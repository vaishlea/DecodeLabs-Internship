What is TriDosha?

Ayurveda explains that every individual is influenced by three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

These energies affect your body, mind, digestion, emotions, sleep, and overall personality.

🌊 Vata

Air + Space. Creative, energetic, adaptable, and imaginative.

🔥 Pitta

Fire + Water. Focused, ambitious, intelligent, and determined.

🌿 Kapha

Earth + Water. Calm, reliable, patient, and nurturing.
